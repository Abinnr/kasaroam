Sri Kodoth Bhagavathy Temple

Sacred and serene – Kodoth Bhagavathy Temple, Kasaragod

1. Where is Kodoth Bhagavathy Temple located?

The Kodoth Bhagavathy Temple is located in the village of Kodom-Belur in Kasaragod district, Kerala. It is approximately 21 kilometers from Kanhangad town and about 2 kilometers from Udanjalil on the Kanhangad–Panathur road, making it easily accessible for pilgrims and visitors.

What is the significance of the temple and its deity?

The temple is dedicated to Goddess Bhagavathy, who represents the divine feminine energy, symbolizing protection and prosperity. Devotees from various regions visit the temple to seek her blessings, especially during the annual festival.

What makes the annual festival at Kodoth Bhagavathy Temple special?

The five-day annual festival, held during the Karthika nakshatra in the Malayalam month of Kumbham (February–March), features vibrant rituals and cultural performances, attracting numerous devotees and tourists while showcasing Kerala’s rich traditions and festive spirit.
