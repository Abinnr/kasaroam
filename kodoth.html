Kodoth Bhagavathy

A Sacred Temple of Devotion & Tradition

Mythology & Significance

Kodoth Bhagavathy Temple is one of the most revered temples in North Kerala, dedicated to Goddess Bhagavathy. It is renowned for its rich spiritual history, traditional Theyyam performances, and deep-rooted cultural significance. The temple attracts thousands of devotees who seek divine blessings, particularly during its grand annual festival.

The temple stands as a testament to centuries-old devotion and rituals, preserving the sacred traditions that have been passed down through generations. The deity is believed to be a powerful protector, bringing prosperity and well-being to her devotees.
