Bekal Fort

Kerala's largest and best preserved fort

History

Built in 1650s by Shivappa Nayaka of Bednore, Bekal Fort is spread over 40 acres. The fort's observation tower offers stunning views of the Arabian Sea.

Architecture

The fort's architecture showcases a blend of military and aesthetic features, with massive walls, secret tunnels, and observation decks.

Visiting Hours

Open daily from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM
Entry Fee: ₹25 for Indians, ₹300 for foreigners
