ASAP Skill Park

About ASAP Community Skill Park

A center for skill development and vocational training in Kasaragod.

Location: Kasaragod, Kerala

Skill Development Focus:

It's a state-of-the-art skill development center in Kasargod, Kerala. The park aims to enhance the employability of young people through vocational training. It operates under the Additional Skill Acquisition Programme (ASAP) Kerala.

Courses and Training:

Offers a diverse range of vocational training programs. Key areas include:

  • Drone Technology (it is a DGCA approved Remote Pilot Training Center)
  • IT and IT-enabled Services
  • Finance
  • Accounting
  • Web development
  • and many more.

They offer training in various sectors like: Capital Goods and Manufacturing, Banking and Finance, Healthcare, and many others.

Key Initiatives:

"Dharppanam" is a special initiative focused on providing skilling programs for women who couldn't pursue higher education. It provides career counseling and placement assistance.

Partnerships:

ASAP partners with leading industrial partners to deliver quality skill training. Linnk academy India is a large operating partner.

Community Role:

Community Skill Parks (CSPs) like the one in Kasargod function as hubs for community-based training. They also host events, workshops, and placement drives.

In essence, ASAP Skill Park Kasargod is a vital resource for young people in the region, providing them with the skills and opportunities they need to succeed in today's job market.
