Edneer Mutt

A Spiritual and Cultural Hub in Kasaragod

Spiritual & Cultural Legacy

Edneer Mutt, a well-known monastic institution in Kasaragod, is a center for cultural and spiritual learning. Rooted in the Dvaita tradition of Hindu philosophy, it has been a beacon of knowledge and devotion for centuries. The Mutt serves as a hub for classical arts, Vedic studies, and social upliftment activities.
